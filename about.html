INFO

I'm an undergraduate student at the University of Michigan, studying Electrical Engineering and graduating in 2027.

I design spaces and systems that people can enjoy, always striving to curate an engaging experience as they navigate the world.

During my sophomore year, I decided to shift my career towards designing for analog computing spaces because of a desire to work at more modern and holistic scales. While computing and architecture slowly evolve, still, at the heart of these disciplines are what makes design valuable – it fulfills people's lives through emotion, functionality, and purpose.
